My Services

Data Collection & Cleaning:

A data analyst gathers raw data from various sources, including databases, APIs, and spreadsheets. They then clean and preprocess the data, removing duplicates, handling missing values, and ensuring data quality for analysis. This step is crucial to ensure accuracy and reliability in further analysis.

Learn more

Data Analysis & Interpretation:

This involves analyzing the cleaned data to uncover patterns, trends, and insights. Using statistical methods and tools like Python, Excel, or SQL, the analyst provides meaningful insights to support decision-making. They may also perform exploratory data analysis (EDA) to understand data distribution and relationships between variables.

Learn more

Data Visualization & Reporting:

Data analysts present their findings through interactive dashboards, reports, and visualizations using tools like Power BI, Tableau, or Excel. This step translates complex data into actionable insights, enabling stakeholders to make informed business decisions. Regular reports and automated dashboards are often provided to track performance and key metrics.
